Glaucoma Treatment Stages

Learn about the stages of glaucoma and available treatments.

Stage 1: Early Glaucoma

In the early stage, regular monitoring and prescription eye drops can slow the progression.

Stage 2: Moderate Glaucoma

More noticeable vision changes occur. Treatment may include laser therapy or stronger medications.

Stage 3: Advanced Glaucoma

Increased vision loss and optic nerve damage. Surgery may be required to relieve intraocular pressure.

Stage 4: End-Stage Glaucoma

Severe vision loss or blindness. The focus is on improving quality of life and symptom management.
